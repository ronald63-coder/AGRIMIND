# AgriMind 🌱🧠

**Africa's Agricultural Intelligence Machine**

AgriMind transforms fragmented agricultural data into actionable intelligence through Knowledge Graphs, AI Agents, and Graph Neural Networks (GNNs).

---

## Overview

Agriculture generates enormous amounts of data every day:

* Weather forecasts
* Satellite imagery
* Soil sensor data
* Research reports
* Market prices
* Extension officer knowledge

Unfortunately, this information exists in separate silos.

Farmers, researchers, policymakers, and agribusinesses often make critical decisions using incomplete information.

AgriMind solves this problem by connecting these fragmented sources into a unified Agricultural Intelligence Machine capable of observing, remembering, reasoning, predicting, and acting.

---

## The Problem

African agriculture suffers from a data fragmentation problem.

A farmer may have access to weather information but not market data.

A researcher may publish important findings that never reach farmers.

A satellite may detect crop stress while local stakeholders remain unaware.

The result is:

* Reduced productivity
* Poor climate resilience
* Delayed responses to pests and diseases
* Inefficient resource allocation

---

## Our Solution

AgriMind integrates multiple agricultural data sources into a centralized intelligence layer.

### Intelligence Pipeline

The platform creates semantic relationships between:

This enables AI systems to understand context instead of processing isolated data points.

## Core Technologies

### Knowledge Graph

The Knowledge Graph acts as the long-term memory of AgriMind.

It models relationships between agricultural entities and events.

Example:

Weather Change → Soil Moisture Reduction → Pest Outbreak → Yield Impact

---

### Graph Neural Networks (GNN)

AgriMind uses Graph Neural Networks to:

* Discover hidden patterns
* Predict risks
* Infer missing information
* Analyze interconnected agricultural systems

The GNN visualization demonstrates how information flows through the agricultural ecosystem.

---

### AI Agents

Specialized AI agents perform:

* Data interpretation
* Agricultural reasoning
* Risk assessment
* Recommendation generation

These agents operate on top of the Knowledge Graph to produce actionable insights.
